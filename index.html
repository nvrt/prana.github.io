Breathe! You are alive!!!

You are the breath of GOD!

“All of humanity's problems stem from man's inability to sit quietly in a room alone.”

Blaise Pascal, scientist and philosopher, 1654

A simple act of focussing on the breath not only helps one sit still, but also assists in helping dissolve all external factors that cause stress in our bodies.
In simple terms Pranayama is a form of Yogic Breathing. It is the pause in the movement of inhalation and exhalation.
